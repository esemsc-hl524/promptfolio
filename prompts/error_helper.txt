## What Happened
This is a SyntaxError on line 1 where Python expected a colon.

## Why It Occurred
Function definitions in Python must end with a colon (:) to indicate where the function body begins.

## How to Fix It
Look at your function definition line and add a colon at the very end, right after the closing parenthesis.

## Prevention Tip
Remember that `def`, `if`, `for`, `while`, and `class` statements always need a colon. Example: `def my_function():`
